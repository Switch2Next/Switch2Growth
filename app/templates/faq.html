FAQ

Answers to Frequently Asked Questions About Building Custom AI Solutions
01 . Can you integrate with our ERP, inventory, and logistics providers?

Yes. We integrate ERPs like Tally, Zoho, Odoo, and Netsuite, sync inventory bi-directionally, and connect to providers like Delhivery, Shiprocket, Shippo, and EasyPost. We design retries, idempotency, and observability to keep data consistent even during spikes.

02 . Do you support India-specific payments and US tax requirements?

Yes. India: UPI, wallets, netbanking, and cards via Razorpay/PayU; GST invoices and HSN codes. US: Stripe/Adyen with ACH and cards; state-level sales tax via TaxJar/Avalara; address validation and UPS/USPS/FedEx shipping. We configure compliance per market and support multi-currency.

03 . How can agentic AI boost conversion rates on our storefront?

Agentic AI can act as an on-site copilot: guiding product discovery, answering questions with retrieval over your catalog and policies, comparing variants, and auto-filling carts. 04 . How do you ensure performance and SEO for ecommerce?

We optimize Core Web Vitals with SSR/ISR, image optimization, and edge caching. Structured data for products, breadcrumbs, and reviews improves discoverability. We generate localized sitemaps, handle canonical URLs for variants, and ensure fast TTFB globally via CDNs.

05 . How do you implement product search and recommendations?

We combine semantic search with vector embeddings for relevance and filters for facets. Recommendations use hybrid approaches: collaborative signals, content-based vectors, and rules for business priorities. For speed, we use Algolia/Elastic/OpenSearch and cache results at the edge.

06 . WHow do you measure success after launch?

We define KPIs up front: conversion, AOV, CAC:LTV, page speed, and support resolution time. Post-launch, we run weekly experiments, monitor funnel analytics, and ship improvements via a roadmap. Agentic systems report actions and outcomes to a shared dashboard.

07 . What does your agentic customer support look like?

A support agent triages tickets, answers FAQs, processes returns, and escalates edge cases. It uses retrieval over your docs, past tickets, and order data. Human-in-the-loop handoff is built-in. We log every action for audit and continuously fine-tune prompts/tools from real conversations.
